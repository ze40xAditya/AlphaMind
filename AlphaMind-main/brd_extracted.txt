Executive Summary 
 
Project Vision 
AlphaMind is an NSE-focused stock intelligence platform that helps retail investors identify 
high-quality investment opportunities through a transparent technical and fundamental ranking 
system.  
 
Problem Statement 
 
Retail investors face difficulty analyzing stocks because financial and market data is scattered, 
complex, and often requires significant expertise to interpret. Existing platforms primarily provide 
raw information rather than actionable insights. AlphaMind addresses this challenge by combining 
key technical and fundamental indicators into a transparent stock-ranking system that helps 
investors quickly identify attractive investment opportunities.  
 
Core Innovation 
AlphaMind introduces a transparent and explainable stock-ranking framework that combines both technical and 
fundamental analysis into a single investment score. Instead of presenting investors with dozens of 
disconnected financial ratios and market indicators, the platform evaluates each stock using four carefully 
selected technical KPIs (RSI, SMA Trend, Momentum, and Volume Trend) and four fundamental KPIs (ROE, 
Debt-to-Equity, Revenue Growth, and EPS Growth). These indicators are weighted and normalized to generate 
Technical and Fundamental Scores, which are then combined into a Final Investment Score ranging from 0 to 
100. This approach enables retail investors to quickly compare stocks, identify high-potential opportunities, and 
make data-driven investment decisions through a simple, objective, and fully explainable ranking methodology.

Geographic Scope & Phased Rollout 
To ensure data pipeline stability and adherence to localized financial exchange regulatory protocols, 
deployment is segmented into distinct geographical market milestones: 
 
Phase 1 (Immediate Market Focus): Indian Stock Market, covering all core equities listed on the 
National Stock Exchange (NSE) and Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE). 
Phase 2 (Global Expansion Architecture): Integration of international exchanges including 
NASDAQ, New York Stock Exchange (NYSE), London Stock Exchange (LSE), and other major 
global financial markets. 
 
Platform & Functional Modules 
Stock Data Ingestion Module 
The Data Ingestion Module serves as the platform's data acquisition layer. It fetches historical price data 
and company financial data for NSE-listed stocks. The module follows a hybrid approach where stocks are 
dynamically fetched and stored upon user requests. The collected data is standardized. 
Technical Analysis Engine (40%) 
The Technical Analysis Engine evaluates market behavior, trend strength, and investor sentiment using 
historical stock price data.

в—ЏвЂ‹ SMA 50 vs SMA 200 (30%) вЂ“ Identifies long-term trend direction.

в—ЏвЂ‹ Volume Trend (20%) вЂ“ Evaluates trading activity and trend confirmation.

Fundamental Analysis Engine (60%) 
The Fundamental Analysis Engine evaluates business quality, financial health, and growth potential.

в—ЏвЂ‹ ROE (30%) вЂ“ Measures profitability and capital efficiency. 
в—ЏвЂ‹ Debt-to-Equity Ratio (25%) вЂ“ Evaluates financial leverage and risk.
